I am an aspiring AI researcher interested in reinforcement learning for large language models and diffusion models, both discrete and continuous. I began research in the field of NLP leveraging linguistic properties to evaluate and train translation models. From then on, my research interests broadened to injecting controllability into different forms of generative models.
